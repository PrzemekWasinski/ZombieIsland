Welcome!

Zombie Island is an online RPG where you can fight enemies, chat with players, explore the world, destroy objects, collect, sell and consume items and buy upgrades to level up!

Controls:

  • W A S D: Walk
  • Space: Attack
  • E: interact with objects
  • I: Open/close inventory
  • T: Start typing a message
  • Escape: Close menu / Cancel typing
  • Left click: Buy/Sell/Consume items in inventory
  • Right click: Discard items from inventory
